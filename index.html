Research Experience

Dark Matter Constraints with Neutron Star Cooling
Apr 2025 – Present
Master's Thesis · LPTHE, Sorbonne Université & IISER TVM
Supervisors: Prof. Silvia Manconi (LPTHE), Prof. Mathew Arun Thomas (IISER TVM), Consultation: Prof. Dany Page (UNAM)
  • Solved the Tolman–Oppenheimer–Volkoff equations for realistic nuclear EoS to obtain structural profiles of DM-admixed neutron stars.
  • Re-derived neutrino emissivity from DM-induced heating at amplitude level; extended calculation to DM-induced neutron decay — a previously undocumented emissivity channel.
  • Implemented both heating mechanisms as a dedicated subroutine inside NSCool; resulting cooling curves exhibit clear deviations from standard neutron-star evolution.
