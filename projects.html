Dark Matter Constraints with Neutron Star Cooling
Master's Thesis · LPTHE, Sorbonne Université & IISER TVM · Apr 2025–Present
  • Solved TOV equations for realistic nuclear EoS to obtain structural profiles of DM-admixed neutron stars.
  • Re-derived neutrino emissivity from DM-induced heating; extended to DM-induced neutron decay — a previously undocumented channel.
  • Implemented both mechanisms inside NSCool; cooling curves show clear deviations from standard evolution.
